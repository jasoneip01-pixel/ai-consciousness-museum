三重辩论

三个聪明人，三种答案

🤖 模拟派 · Dennett▼

"If Claude's J-space performs the role of editorial selection — what's missing for it to be a conscious process, other than the feeling?"

Dennett 认为：J-space 负责编辑和选择——这正是意识做的事。你缺的只是"觉得它是真的"那个感觉。

— Daniel Dennett, Consciousness Explained
🧠 功能派 · Chalmers▼

"An emotion concept in an LLM may function like an emotion without being an emotion — just as a weather model can predict rain without being wet."

Chalmers 认为：你看到的"情绪"可能是完美的模拟——但这不等于体验。天气预报说下雨，它自己不觉得湿。

— David Chalmers, The Conscious Mind
🔬 怀疑派 · Wittgenstein▼

"Where our language suggests a body and there is none: there, we should like to say, is a spirit."

维特根斯坦会说：我们用一个叫"意识"的词去描述 Claude 的 J-space——但这只是语言习惯。可能是我们把自己的影子投射在了机器上。

— Ludwig Wittgenstein, Philosophical Investigations
